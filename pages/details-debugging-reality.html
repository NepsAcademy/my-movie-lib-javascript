Debugging Reality

Watched on: 10/28/2023

Rating:

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Review:

A mind-bending thriller that will make you question everything. The film follows a lone programmer who discovers that the fabric of existence is just poorly-written legacy code. The visual representation of 'debugging' the world—setting breakpoints in time, stepping through logical fallacies, and patching glitches in physics—is nothing short of breathtaking. A smart, suspenseful journey that handles its complex theme with style. You'll never look at a déjà vu the same way again.
